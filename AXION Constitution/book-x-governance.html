Preamble

Governance as Stewardship

Power without accountability eventually becomes corruption. Innovation without governance eventually becomes chaos. Growth without ethics eventually becomes exploitation.

History demonstrates that civilizations rarely collapse because they lacked intelligence. They collapse because they failed to govern intelligence wisely.

AXION recognizes governance not as bureaucracy, but as stewardship.
Governance preserves purpose.
Protects people.
Allocates authority.
Reduces unnecessary risk.
Safeguards institutional memory.
Maintains public trust.

Most importantly, governance ensures that the mission remains greater than any individual. Not even the Founder stands above the Constitution. The Constitution governs all.
